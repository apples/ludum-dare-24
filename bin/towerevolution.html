Tower Evolution


Click on the upgrades in the bottom-left to select one and click on a tower to apply it. Not all upgrades are compatible.

Click on the icons above the upgrade bar to shuffle it. You get 5 shuffles on each level.

The bar at the top is your progress meter. When it is empty, enemies stop spawning. When all enemies are killed, you advance to the next level. There are 5 levels.

Red towers build faster, square towers fire faster
You can change existing towers' shapes
You can add guns to a tower and upgrade the tower's range and power
Support towers increase the fire rate of all surrounding towers
Mortars are slow, but cause powerful explosions
Lasers deal continuous damage
Pulse towers hit all enemies within a short distance
